Thanks for Downloading Blox.
Here's how to use this font:

'UPPERCASE' letters slant \   [pressing Shift]
'lowercase' letters slant /


To make the 'Numbers' slant \  press Shift + the 'Number'

[---  SO for 0-9 slanting \, you would use )!@#$%^&*(  ---]
